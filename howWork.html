Weekly Monthly

The weekly meetings held with individual cultural and social identity will be run with a structure similar to that of Info for Better Tomorrow If any club or organization plans to present on a certain topic, for example a misconception in regards to their culture or social identity, the club will present on their topic first and then a discussion portion is held with Q/A.

Each representative holds weekly meetings/sub-meetings for each month individually with a culture or subgroup.
When
Individual meetings can be a hybrid of presentations to share information and resources or request for resources.
Format

During monthly meetings, representatives from all districts gather to talk about some updates they have within their district on an ongoing project, discuss any requests clubs or organizations have made, and help connect districts or representatives with clubs who can help provide support and resources.

During all the meetings, teachers and educators should be encouraged and informed about them, so that they have the opportunity to learn about the discussions and participate in them if interested.
Community
During monthly meetings with all the representatives, create an interconnected web of support between different districts and raise concerns or suggestions in general in regards to the project to help improve it.
Support
An example of how a support system would work includes the support and connection of a larger and more organized club or organization (e.g. a well-known Black Student Union) to a smaller and more new club or organization (e.g. a new and small Black Student Union or Asian Student Union) to help facilitate a support system and sharing of ideas and resources. This can be done in the Slack and Discord channels.
